I have a few features that I have been thinking about for fediview:

  • Only include posts of a particular language (now available on your profile)
  • Send daily/weekly emails with Mastodon summaries
  • Get a summary of tags or lists
  • "If this, than that" style workflows (e.g. when a post with a certain tag is in my home timeline, automatically bookmark it)
  • Store an archival of digests (in a way that is acceptable to the Mastodon ethos)
  • Always include posts from certain accounts in the summary
  • Schedule posts
  • Able to boost or favorite a post directly
  • Follow users directly
  • See replies to a post
  • See whether a post is part of a thread
  • Surface posts by users who haven't posted in a while
